Volunteer Opportunities

There are opportunities for us to help female representation in the media all over! Let's look at some examples.

  1. Schools
    • Call your local elementary school and lok for opportunities to sub in art classes!
    • Offer to present on women in media, or media in general.
    • Help judge or host events that encourage a love for the arts in children.
  2. Support the WGA
    • Donate to those on the picket line.
    • March with the WGA!
    • Share information and updates about the strike with those you know.
  3. Donation Opportunities
    • Donate to the WGA
    • Keep the arts in schools
    • Support scholarships for minority women in film school.
